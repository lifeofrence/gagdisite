OUR MISSION

Our mission is to make positive changes in the lives of the less privileged members of the society by promoting unity and peaceful coexistence, good governance, health and education development, youth empowerment and social welfare to generally break the cycle of poverty.

OUR VALUES

Gagdi support foundation is non-profit, philanthropic support organisation founded by Rt Hon. Yusuf Adamu Gagdi to support the less privilege members of the society and promote unity and peaceful coexistence, health and educational development, good governance, youth empowerment, and social welfare among many others

OUR VISION

We envision a society devoid of human degradation and exploitation where the less privileged members can aspire to any level and achieve any dream goal by providing opportunities to realise their full potential through philanthropic support.

Giving is Virtue.

Far from the lands of hardship and injustice, beyond the barriers of inequality, there exists a beacon of hope - the Gagdi Support Foundation, dedicated to uplifting the lives of the less privileged through unity, peace, education, and healthcare.

WHAT WE DO

Empowering communities through peacebuilding, education, healthcare, environmental sustainability, legal aid, poverty alleviation, and skill development.

WHY WE DO WHAT WE DO

It is out of the genuine desire to make positive changes in the lives of less privileged members of our society, for until you touch lives positively, one has not lived.

WHO WE ARE
