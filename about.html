WHAT WE DO

Empowering communities through peacebuilding, education, healthcare, environmental sustainability, legal aid, poverty alleviation, and skill development

Our Initiatives

Through a diverse range of impactful programs, we are committed to addressing the pressing needs of our communities. From facilitating peace talks to resolving conflicts, fostering educational development and excellence, conducting medical outreach and sensitization campaigns, promoting sustainable environmental practices, providing free legal services to marginalized populations, distributing palliatives to alleviate hardship, and offering capacity building and vocational training for self-reliance, each initiative is a step towards a brighter, more equitable future. By engaging in these programs, we aim to empower individuals, uplift communities, and create lasting positive change.

OUR GOAL AND OBJECTIVES

Our goals and objectives are deeply rooted in our commitment to creating a better society for all. We strive to assist and support the less privileged members of our society, promoting peace, unity, and harmonious co-existence among communities. Through empowering youth with vocational trainings, skill acquisition, and financial support, we aim to foster their business development and self-reliance. Additionally, we are dedicated to promoting educational development and excellence through scholarship grants and sponsorships. Our focus on good governance includes advocating for transparency and accountability, ensuring efficient and responsible governance practices. We also prioritize healthcare development and awareness, working towards a healthier community. By mobilizing communities, we seek to encourage active participation in socio-economic development, including initiatives in health, education, and environmental conservation.

OUR VALUES

At Gagdi Support Foundation, our values form the foundation of everything we do. Our commitment to being NON-PARTISAN ensures that we serve all those in need with impartiality and equity. Through ADVOCACY, we empower the most vulnerable members of our society to participate in decisions that shape their community. We embrace INNOVATION, constantly seeking new solutions to improve the standard of living in our society. Lastly, we hold ourselves to the highest standards of INTEGRITY, remaining truthful, honest, and respectful in all our endeavors, upholding the reputation of the Gagdi Support Foundation.
